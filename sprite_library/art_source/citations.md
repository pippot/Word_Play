# Sprite Library Citations and Credits

This document tracks the origin and licensing of the pixel art assets used in this library.

## Characters

### FREE Mana Seed Character Base Demo 2.0
* **Author:** Seliel the Shaper / Mana Seed
* **Contents:** Base character sprites (`src/characters/humanoids/base`) and example weapons (`src/items/equipment/weapons`).
* **Source:** https://seliel-the-shaper.itch.io/character-base

## Items

### FreePixelFood
* **Contents:** Various pixel art food items (`src/items/consumables/food`).
* **Source:** Downloaded asset pack 'FreePixelFood'.

## World Tiles, VFX, and Miscellaneous

### DawnLike
* **Author:** DragonDePlatino and DawnHack contributors
* **Contents:** Characters, enemies, tiles, and items (`src/characters/*`, `src/world_tiles/*`, `src/items/*`, `src/vfx/*`).
* **License:** typically CC-BY-SA 3.0 / CC0 depending on specific tile, part of the extensive DawnLike universal roguelike tileset.
* **Source:** https://opengameart.org/content/dawnlike-16x16-universal-rogue-like-tileset-v181

## Food & Consumables

### LPC Food v2
* **Author:** bluecarrot16, Daniel Eddeland (daneeklu), Joshua Taylor, Richard Kettering (Jetrel), thekingphoenix, RedVoxel, and Molly "Cougarmint" Willits
* **Contents:** Fruits, vegetables, bread, pastry, and animal products
* **License:** CC-BY-SA 3.0 / GPL 3.0+ (dual licensed)
* **Source:** https://opengameart.org/content/lpc-food
* **Location:** `src/items/consumables/lpc_food/`

## Environment Tiles

### Kenney Tiny Dungeon
* **Author:** Kenney (www.kenney.nl)
* **Contents:** 132 dungeon/environment tiles (16x16) including floors, walls, stairs, doors, chests
* **License:** CC0 (public domain)
* **Source:** https://kenney.nl/assets/tiny-dungeon
* **Location:** `src/world_tiles/outdoors/kenney_tiny_dungeon/`

### CDDA-Tilesets
* **Author:** Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead contributors (I-am-Erk, etc.)
* **Contents:** Various appliance sprites (`src/world_tiles/indoors/appliances/CDDA/*`).
* **License:** CC BY-SA 3.0
* **Source:** https://github.com/I-am-Erk/CDDA-Tilesets

### Pokemon-gen-2-style-tilemap
* **Author:** nikouu
* **Contents:** Gen 2 style indoor appliances and furniture (`src/world_tiles/indoors/appliances/PokemonGen2/*`).
* **Source:** https://github.com/nikouu/Pokemon-gen-2-style-tilemap

### Custom Sprites (Blacksmith Environment)
* **Contents:** Custom created sprites for the blacksmith environment:
  - `src/world_tiles/indoors/stations/forge.png` - Custom forge/anvil station sprite
  - `src/items/materials/misc/iron_ore.png` - Custom iron ore sprite
  - `src/world_tiles/indoors/floors/brick_floor.png` - Custom brick floor tile
* **License:** Custom, based on DawnLike style
